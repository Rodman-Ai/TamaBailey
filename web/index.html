TamaBailey

A virtual hound dog you can keep alive in your browser.

You're watching a friend's Bailey. Actions disabled.

How to play

  • Short-press / tap a button to Feed, Play, or Clean.
  • Hold any button for ~0.8 s to open the status menu. Tap a stat / press a button again to cycle tabs.
  • Tap the dog on the canvas to pet Bailey, or drag across him for continuous strokes.
  • Keyboard: A / B / C; hold for menu; N next tab; R restart when Bailey is gone.
  • Bailey grows up over real time: 24 h healthy → Adult, 96 h → Senior. Neglect kills him.
  • State is saved in localStorage. Share Bailey generates a URL friends can open to see your pup.

Same game runs on a Waveshare ESP32-S3-Touch-LCD-1.54. See the repo README.
