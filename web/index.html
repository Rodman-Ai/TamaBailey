TamaBailey

A virtual hound dog you can keep alive in your browser.

How to play

  • Short-press / tap a button to Feed, Play, or Clean.
  • Hold any button for ~0.8 s to open the status menu.
  • Tap the dog on the canvas to pet Bailey (small happiness bump, 12 s cooldown).
  • Keyboard: A / B / C, hold for menu, R to restart if Bailey is gone.
  • State is saved in localStorage -- come back later and Bailey is still here.

Same game runs on a Waveshare ESP32-S3-Touch-LCD-1.54 device. See the repo README.
